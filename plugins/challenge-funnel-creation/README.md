# CHALLENGE FUNNEL CREATION

## Tags
CHALLENGE FUNNELS, 5-DAY CHALLENGE, LEAD GENERATION, COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT, PRODUCT LAUNCH, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill provides a comprehensive framework for designing and launching a successful 5-day challenge funnel that takes participants through daily tasks culminating in a quick win and offer.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Structures daily content that delivers quick wins and builds momentum
- Creates community engagement strategies to foster participant interaction
- Develops pitch sequences that naturally transition to your paid offer
- Plans pre-launch hype and post-challenge follow-up campaigns

**When to Use:** Use when launching a new product, building an email list, or wanting to create an engaged community around your brand.
