# AFFILIATE LEADERBOARDS

## Tags
AFFILIATE MARKETING, GAMIFICATION, CONTEST DESIGN, PARTNER MOTIVATION, SALES CONTESTS, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill provides a comprehensive framework for creating and managing highly effective affiliate leaderboards that motivate partners to perform at their best.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Designs compelling prize structures that motivate different affiliate tiers
- Creates real-time tracking systems to maintain competitive energy
- Structures tier-based rewards so everyone has something to play for
- Plans communication cadences to keep affiliates engaged throughout the contest

**When to Use:** Use when launching a product promotion and want to maximize affiliate participation and sales through friendly competition.
