# COMPLIANCE LEGAL

## Tags
MARKETING COMPLIANCE, LEGAL REVIEW, GDPR, FTC GUIDELINES, PRIVACY POLICY, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill helps you navigate the legal complexities of digital marketing, ensuring your practices are ethical and compliant with regulations.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Reviews marketing materials for FTC compliance and truthfulness
- Ensures data handling practices meet GDPR and CCPA requirements
- Verifies email marketing compliance with CAN-SPAM regulations
- Guides proper endorsement and testimonial disclosures

**When to Use:** Use when reviewing campaigns for compliance, updating privacy policies, or ensuring advertising claims are properly substantiated.
