# BIG IDEA DEVELOPMENT

## Tags
MARKETING STRATEGY, BIG IDEA, TODD BROWN, UNIQUE MECHANISM, CAMPAIGN MESSAGING, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill empowers you to develop a compelling and unique marketing Big Idea for your products or services using the E5 methodology created by Todd Brown.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Analyzes market sophistication to position your message appropriately
- Discovers your unique mechanism that makes your promise believable
- Crafts a compelling Big Idea that captures attention and curiosity
- Creates campaign themes that resonate with your target audience beliefs

**When to Use:** Use when launching a new product, entering a competitive market, or when your current messaging is not converting.
