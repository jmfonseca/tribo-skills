# REPUTATION MANAGEMENT

## Tags
ONLINE REPUTATION, REVIEW MANAGEMENT, BRAND PROTECTION, CRISIS MANAGEMENT, SENTIMENT, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill helps manage and improve a business online reputation, protecting and enhancing brand image across platforms.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Monitors online mentions and reviews across platforms
- Responds to negative reviews professionally
- Implements strategies to generate positive reviews
- Manages reputation crises effectively

**When to Use:** Use when managing online reviews, addressing reputation issues, or building proactive reputation strategies.
