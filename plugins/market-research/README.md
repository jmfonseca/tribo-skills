# MARKET RESEARCH

## Tags
MARKET RESEARCH, CUSTOMER INTERVIEWS, COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS, SOCIAL LISTENING, CONSUMER INSIGHTS, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill enables Claude to conduct comprehensive market research, including customer interviews, competitor analysis, and social media listening.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Designs and conducts customer interview programs
- Analyzes competitor positioning and messaging
- Monitors social media for trends and sentiment
- Synthesizes insights into actionable recommendations

**When to Use:** Use when entering new markets, launching products, or needing to understand customer needs more deeply.
