## Description
This skill helps you define, document, and deploy a consistent and authentic brand voice that builds trust and recognition across all communications.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Analyzes audience preferences to inform voice development
- Creates comprehensive voice guidelines with examples
- Documents tone variations for different contexts and channels
- Provides practical frameworks for maintaining voice consistency

**When to Use:** Use when establishing brand guidelines, onboarding new team members, or ensuring consistency across marketing channels.
