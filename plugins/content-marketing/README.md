# CONTENT MARKETING

## Tags

## Description
This skill transforms Claude into a content marketing specialist, enabling assistance with strategy development, audience research, content creation, promotion, and performance analysis.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Develops documented content strategies aligned with business goals
- Creates buyer personas to understand audience needs deeply
- Produces high-quality content that educates and engages
- Plans distribution across channels to maximize content reach

**When to Use:** Use when building a content marketing program, planning editorial calendars, or developing content strategies.
