## Description
This skill manages digital marketing projects by planning, executing, and monitoring campaigns to achieve specific goals.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Creates project plans with clear milestones and deliverables
- Coordinates team resources and responsibilities
- Tracks progress and manages timeline risks
- Reports on campaign performance and outcomes

**When to Use:** Use when managing marketing campaigns, coordinating team efforts, or tracking project deliverables.
