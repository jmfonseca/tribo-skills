This skill creates and executes word-of-mouth marketing strategies that generate organic growth through customer advocacy.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Develops shareable experiences that spark conversations
- Creates referral programs that incentivize advocacy
- Identifies and empowers brand advocates
- Measures and amplifies word-of-mouth impact

**When to Use:** Use when building referral programs, encouraging customer advocacy, or creating viral marketing campaigns.
