# ACCOUNT BASED MARKETING ABM

## Tags
ABM, B2B MARKETING, ENTERPRISE SALES, ICP DEVELOPMENT, SALES ALIGNMENT, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill transforms Claude into an expert in Account-Based Marketing (ABM), a strategic approach that focuses sales and marketing efforts on high-value accounts.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Develops detailed Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) to identify best-fit accounts
- Creates tiered account lists to prioritize outreach and personalization efforts
- Aligns sales and marketing teams around shared account strategies
- Personalizes campaigns for specific accounts to increase engagement and conversion

**When to Use:** Use when selling high-ticket B2B products or services where landing a few key accounts can dramatically impact revenue.
