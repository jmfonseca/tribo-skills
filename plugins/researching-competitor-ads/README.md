# RESEARCHING COMPETITOR ADS

## Tags
COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS, AD RESEARCH, COMPETITOR INTELLIGENCE, CREATIVE ANALYSIS, MARKET RESEARCH, JANUARY_IMPORT

## Description
This skill analyzes competitor ad strategies, deconstructing their campaigns to provide strategic insights for your own advertising.

**How This Skill Helps:**
- Identifies competitor ad placements and spending patterns
- Deconstructs competitor creative and messaging
- Discovers winning angles and approaches in your market
- Finds gaps and opportunities competitors are missing

**When to Use:** Use when planning ad campaigns, seeking creative inspiration, or understanding competitive positioning.
